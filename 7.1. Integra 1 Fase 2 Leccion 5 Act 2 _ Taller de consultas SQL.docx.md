   CON AYUDA DE CHAT GPT:  
   **SELECT CustomerId, FirstName, LastName, SUM(Total) AS TotalSpent**  
   **FROM Invoice**  
   **JOIN Customer ON Invoice.CustomerId \= Customer.CustomerId**  
   **GROUP BY CustomerId**  
   **ORDER BY TotalSpent DESC**  
   **LIMIT 1;**
